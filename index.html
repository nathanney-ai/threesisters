Our Story

Hi! We're Emily, Molly, and Katarina, the sisters behind Three Sisters Book Club.

Since launching Three Sisters Book Club in Kansas City in 2022, our mission has been simple: create a welcoming space where book lovers can gather for laughter, lively debate, and the occasional passionate defense of a fictional character. We want to create a community filled with friendships that last longer than your TBR list.

From author events and book club nights to our beloved Bibliobus and other literary adventures, we are endlessly grateful for every reader who shows up. Thank you for bringing your thoughtful takes, your honest ratings, and for enthusiastically participating when we absolutely insist on casting the hypothetical movie adaptation.

We believe books are better when shared and we're so glad you're part of our story.

Katarina, Molly, and Emily
